How iFindLoan Works: From One Request to a Real Offer

iFindLoan is a free matching service for personal loans from $100 to $5,000. Here is exactly what happens from the moment you start your request to the day funds could land in your account.

iFindLoan is not a lender. We are a loan-matching service that takes one short request and shares it with lenders and lending partners in our network. Instead of applying to ten different sites and hoping one responds, you fill out a single secure form and let interested lenders come to you.

The three steps

1. Tell us what you need

Start the form on our home page and answer a few quick questions — how much you want to request (anywhere from $100 to $5,000), what it is for, and basic details a lender needs to consider you. It takes about two minutes. There is no paperwork to upload and no branch to visit.

2. Compare the options that come back

Your request is passed in real time to lenders in the network. If a lender is interested, you are connected to their offer. Because more than one lender may respond, you can compare what is in front of you before committing to anything.

3. You pick the winner

Review the lender's rate, term, and total cost. If it works for you, you continue with that lender, e-sign their documents, and they fund the loan. If it does not, you walk away. You are never obligated to accept an offer.

Who writes the loan? The lender does. iFindLoan does not set your rate, approve or deny your request, or fund the loan. Every rate, fee, and term is determined solely by the lender that makes you an offer.

What you need to get started

Requirements vary by lender, but most lenders in the network look for applicants who:

  • Are at least 18 years old (19 in some states) and a U.S. resident;
  • Have a steady, verifiable source of income;
  • Hold an active checking account for deposit and repayment; and
  • Can provide a valid phone number and email address.

All credit types are welcome to submit a request. Lenders in the network often weigh more than a credit score — including income and employment — but submitting a request does not guarantee an offer or approval.

How fast can it be?

Completing the form takes roughly two minutes. If you are matched and you accept an offer, many lenders deposit funds by direct deposit as soon as the next business day. Exact timing depends on the lender and your bank's processing.

What it costs you

Nothing. iFindLoan is always free to consumers. We may be compensated by lenders when we connect you — you can read exactly how on our Advertising Disclosure page — but that never adds a fee to your loan or to you.

Tribal lenders are subject to tribal and certain federal laws while being immune from state law including usury caps. If you are connected to a tribal lender, please understand that the tribal lender's rates and fees may be higher than state-licensed lenders.

The purpose of shorter duration loans is to provide the borrower temporary financial relief. Such loans are not a long-term financial solution. Persons facing serious financial difficulties should consider other alternatives or should seek out professional financial advice.
