Privacy Policy

How iFindLoan collects, uses, shares, and protects your information — and the choices you have.

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how iFindLoan.com ("iFindLoan," "we," "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website and loan-matching service. By using the site, you agree to this Policy.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide: such as your name, contact details, address, date of birth, income, employment, bank account details, and identifiers you enter into our forms.
  • Sensitive identifiers: such as your Social Security number or driver license number, when needed to process or verify your request.
  • Automatic information: such as IP address, device and browser type, pages viewed, and referring URLs, collected through cookies and similar technologies.

How we use information

  • To match your request with lenders and lending partners in our network;
  • To operate, secure, and improve our service;
  • To communicate with you, including marketing where permitted; and
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

How we share information

We share the information you submit with lenders, lending partners, and service providers who help us operate. We may also share information with marketing partners, or as required by law, in connection with a business transfer, or to protect our rights. Some sharing may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" under certain state laws; you can opt out (see below).

Your California privacy rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used and shared, the right to request deletion, the right to correct, and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, use our Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information page or email info@ifindloan.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights. See our California Privacy Notice for full detail.

Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for site operation, analytics, attribution, and marketing. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect site functionality.

Data security & retention

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards — including 256-bit SSL encryption in transit — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations.

Children's privacy

Our service is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes & contact

We may update this Policy from time to time; the "last updated" date reflects the latest version. Questions? Email info@ifindloan.com or write to iFindLoan, 1317 Edgewater Dr #2659, Orlando, FL 32804.

Important Disclosures. Please Read Carefully.

This service is not available in all states. If you request a loan in a particular state where such loans are prohibited, or in a location where ifindloan.com does not have an available lender, you will not be connected to a lender. In some cases, you may be given the option of obtaining a loan from a tribal lender. Tribal lenders are subject to tribal and certain federal laws while being immune from state law including usury caps. If you are connected to a tribal lender, please understand that the tribal lender's rates and fees may be higher than state-licensed lenders.

The purpose of shorter duration loans is to provide the borrower temporary financial relief. Such loans are not a long-term financial solution. Persons facing serious financial difficulties should consider other alternatives or should seek out professional financial advice.

This website is not an offer to lend. ifindloan.com is not a lender and does not make loan or credit decisions. ifindloan.com connects interested persons with a lender from its network of approved lenders. ifindloan.com does not control and is not responsible for the actions or inactions of any lender. Regardless, ifindloan.com's service is always free to you.

By submitting your information via this website, you are authorizing ifindloan.com and its partners to do a credit check, which may include verifying your social security number, driver license number or other identification, and a review of your creditworthiness.

Lender's Disclosure of Terms.

The lender you are connected to will provide documents that contain all fees and rate information pertaining to the loan being offered. Loan fees and interest rates are determined solely by the lender based on the lender's internal policies, underwriting criteria and applicable law.

Late Payments Hurt Your Credit Score

Please be aware that missing a payment or making a late payment can negatively impact your credit score.
