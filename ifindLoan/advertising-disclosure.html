Advertising Disclosure

Transparency about how iFindLoan makes money — because you deserve to know before you use a free service.

Last updated: July 8, 2026

iFindLoan.com is a free service for consumers. We do not charge you to submit a request or to be connected with a lender. To keep the service free, iFindLoan is compensated by the lenders and lending partners in our network.

How we are paid

When we connect your request with a lender or lending partner, that company may pay iFindLoan a fee for the referral. In many cases, compensation is based on a marketplace or "ping-tree" model — similar in concept to how ad marketplaces work — where the highest available bidder for a matching request may be connected with you. This compensation supports our operations and allows us to offer the service at no cost to you.

How this affects what you see

Compensation may influence which lenders or offers appear and in what order. It does not change the price of any loan you are offered, and iFindLoan never adds a fee to your loan. iFindLoan does not set rates, approve or deny requests, or fund loans — every rate, fee, and term is set solely by the lender.

Being matched with a lender is not an endorsement of that lender, and it is not a guarantee of approval or of any particular rate. Always review a lender's full terms and only accept a loan you can afford to repay.

Your choices

You control your information. You can opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information, review our Privacy Policy, and unsubscribe from communications at any time. Questions? Email info@ifindloan.com.

Important Disclosures. Please Read Carefully.

This service is not available in all states. If you request a loan in a particular state where such loans are prohibited, or in a location where ifindloan.com does not have an available lender, you will not be connected to a lender. In some cases, you may be given the option of obtaining a loan from a tribal lender. Tribal lenders are subject to tribal and certain federal laws while being immune from state law including usury caps. If you are connected to a tribal lender, please understand that the tribal lender's rates and fees may be higher than state-licensed lenders.

The purpose of shorter duration loans is to provide the borrower temporary financial relief. Such loans are not a long-term financial solution. Persons facing serious financial difficulties should consider other alternatives or should seek out professional financial advice.

This website is not an offer to lend. ifindloan.com is not a lender and does not make loan or credit decisions. ifindloan.com connects interested persons with a lender from its network of approved lenders. ifindloan.com does not control and is not responsible for the actions or inactions of any lender. Regardless, ifindloan.com's service is always free to you.

By submitting your information via this website, you are authorizing ifindloan.com and its partners to do a credit check, which may include verifying your social security number, driver license number or other identification, and a review of your creditworthiness.

Lender's Disclosure of Terms.

The lender you are connected to will provide documents that contain all fees and rate information pertaining to the loan being offered. Loan fees and interest rates are determined solely by the lender based on the lender's internal policies, underwriting criteria and applicable law.

Late Payments Hurt Your Credit Score

Please be aware that missing a payment or making a late payment can negatively impact your credit score.
