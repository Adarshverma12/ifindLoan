Common questions

Answered before you had to ask

How fast can I get funded?

If a lender in the network approves your request and you accept the offer, funds are often deposited as soon as the next business day. Exact timing depends on the lender and your bank.

Does checking my options affect my credit?

Submitting your request to iFindLoan does not affect your credit score. A lender may run a credit check once you continue with a specific offer — that step, and its impact, is disclosed by the lender before you agree.

Can I qualify with bad credit?

All credit types are welcome to submit a request. Lenders in the network weigh more than a score, including income and employment. Submitting a request does not guarantee an offer or approval.

How much does iFindLoan cost?

Nothing. iFindLoan is always free to you. We are not a lender — we connect your request with lenders who may compensate us for the referral. Your lender discloses all loan costs before you sign.

Is iFindLoan a lender?

No. iFindLoan is not a lender and does not make loan or credit decisions. We connect interested people with a lender from our network of approved lenders. The lender writes the offer and sets the rate.

How much can I request?

You can request between $100 and $5,000. The amount you are offered depends on the lender, your state, and your financial profile.
