Please read this Disclaimer carefully before using iFindLoan.com.

Not a lender

iFindLoan.com is not a lender and does not make loan or credit decisions. iFindLoan connects interested persons with a lender from its network of approved lenders. iFindLoan does not control and is not responsible for the actions or inactions of any lender. Regardless, iFindLoan's service is always free to you.

Not available in all states

This service is not available in all states. If you request a loan where such loans are prohibited, or where iFindLoan does not have an available lender, you will not be connected to a lender. In some cases you may be offered a loan from a tribal lender, whose rates and fees may be higher than state-licensed lenders.

Not a long-term solution

The purpose of shorter duration loans is to provide temporary financial relief. Such loans are not a long-term financial solution. Persons facing serious financial difficulties should consider other alternatives or seek professional financial advice.

Rates and terms set by lenders

The lender you are connected to will provide documents that contain all fees and rate information pertaining to the loan being offered. Loan fees and interest rates are determined solely by the lender based on the lender's internal policies, underwriting criteria, and applicable law.

Late payments

Missing a payment or making a late payment can negatively impact your credit score. Only accept loan terms you can afford to repay, and contact your lender immediately if you cannot make a payment on time.

No professional advice

Information on this Site is general and educational; it is not financial, legal, or tax advice. Consult a qualified professional for guidance specific to your situation.
